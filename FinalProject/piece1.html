LATINO NETART DATABASE
Within the framework of map.jodi, this work of net art provides an engrossing illustration of a programming idea in relation to databases on an HTML webpage. It cleverly uses an eye-catching HTML interface to vividly illustrate the complexities of database administration. The dynamic database that is depicted in the artwork serves as a visual representation of how databases work in the context of web development, as well as an instructional tool. With their creative portrayal of properties and relationships, map.jodi's nostalgic aesthetics pay homage to the historical era of relational schemas and SQL. Users get an immersive experience that mimics the features of actual databases in addition to being visually engaged by the dynamic HTML components.
